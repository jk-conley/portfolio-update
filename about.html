Hobbies

When I'm not coding or learning a new skill, I enjoy traveling and trying different foods. The experience of meeting new people in another culture and trying their food is a mind blowing moment that cannot be forgotten.

Below is a list of some places and foods I have had the opportunity to enjoy:

  • China, Fujian Province: fish head and steamed leeks
  • Mexico, Rocky Point: manaray soft shell tacos
  • USA, Maine: (Grandma's home cooking) steamed clams with hot butter and lobster
  • USA, Texas: BBQ brisket and home made mashed potatoes
  • USA: Arizona: Sonoran hot dogs (hot dog wrapped in bacon with beans, mayo, and more)
